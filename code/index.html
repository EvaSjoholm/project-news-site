    WELCOME 2023 - JANUARY

    It’s 2023 and the Skydiving community is upp and running as normal in the south of Europe.

    Due to weather being uneasy during December month, a lot of camps and courses for beginners will continue in January and February month.

    Skydive SPAIN had a successfull XMAS BOOGIE with over 200 people! They broke theire record for amount of jumps during one day

    and it was a real hit for all the fun-jumpers! Skydive ALGARVE had a great turnout of their NEW YEARS party with fireworks close to the bar, live DJ:s under the stars

    and people visiting from all over just to welcome the new year.

    This is a period where most Dropzones are in the plannings for the season whilst others continue with both AFF-courses, camps and so forth. For those in northern Europe the focus during this period is mostly tunnel-flying. Both in Sweden and in Poland some top coaches from around the world are available for coaching So be on the look-out for coaching, you will find updateed info down below under ‘AFF / TUNNEL’.
